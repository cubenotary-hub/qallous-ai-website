# Sage CEO - Fortune 500 Strategic Advisor

## Overview

Sage is Qallous AI's CEO + Strategic Advisor AI partner, delivering Fortune 500-level strategic guidance powered by a sophisticated RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) system.

## Features

- **145,000+ words** of Fortune 500 CEO knowledge
- **30+ years** of executive experience
- **15 IPOs** led successfully
- **$50B+ in M&A** transactions
- **Real-time strategic advice** via voice or text

1. **IPO Preparation** - 24-month playbook from decision to Day 180
2. **M&A Strategy** - Buy-side and sell-side complete frameworks
3. **Crisis Management** - Hour-by-hour response plans for all crisis types
4. **Strategic Frameworks** - Porter's Five Forces, SWOT, Blue Ocean, OKRs, BCG Matrix
5. **Leadership & Culture** - Team building, hiring, board management
